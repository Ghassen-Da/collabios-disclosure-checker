    What a compliant disclosure looks like

    Across the EU and UK, sponsored content must be labelled clearly, upfront and in plain language — so the average viewer understands it is advertising before they engage. The exact wording differs by market:

    UK — “Ad”, “#ad”, the platform Paid partnership label (ASA / CMA).
    France — “collaboration commerciale” or “publicité”, in French; France passed a dedicated influencer-marketing law in 2023.
    Italy — “#adv”, “#pubblicità” or “in collaborazione con” (AGCM / AGCom guidance).
    Germany — “Werbung” or “Anzeige”, clearly visible.
    Spain — “publicidad” / “patrocinado”.
    Netherlands — “advertentie” / “betaalde samenwerking”.

    Weak or ambiguous labels — “#sp”, “#collab”, “ambassador”, “gifted”, “thanks to…”, or an “#ad” buried at the end of a long hashtag block — are widely treated as not sufficient. Use a recognised term and put it near the start.
